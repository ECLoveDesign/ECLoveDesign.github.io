About FE62

FE62: Making Financial Knowledge Inclusive

Under the direction of the FSC, TABF has created the first museum of finance! Completed in late 2022, it is located on the third floor of the Taiwan Academy of Banking and Finance (TABF), No. 62, Section 3, Roosevelt Rd.

The museum's name, Financial Explorer 62, reflects the fusion of the financial sector's spirit of exploration and TABF's talent training base.

 
 
First Floor Bankers' Salon: A Salon for Collaboration and Rest

The first floor is meant to provide services especially for those employed in the finance industry. Currently, course participants and testers can use the QR code in the members app to enter the Salon for free!

The area beyond the entry gate does not have any additional spaces. However, modular furniture employing a curated concept of flexibility provides an agile and flexible space.

The multi-use media wall is well-suited for any kind of exchange, whether deep discussions and bookclubs or media interviews. The bright, high-ceiling lighting and comfortable, green environment are perfect for both individual rest and for preparing for the next stop on your educational journey.

This floor also features an exclusive work based on an artistic intrepretation of the meaning of financial policy. Additionally, the "Explorers' Coffee Cart" and digital vending machines, which are primarily paid for via membership points, provide a digital atmosphere and a good setting for the use of membership points!

 
 
Third Floor FE62: A Space for Exploring Finance

FE62's historical collection and a variety of media display the trajectory and achievements of Taiwanese finance. Moreover, many interactive programs simulating the financial paths of daily life help to broaden audiences' financial horizons and build proper attitudes towards finance.

The space is used for the exploration of financial knowledge, with the circular corridor making up the mobile game path. Use this game to challenge your knowledge of the exhibits' information on finance!
